Introduction


In this portfolio I showcase some of my major achievements so far, developing a self designed city generator, NPC AI based on Utility Behavioural Trees, a first person shooter game, and an implementation of projector effects; all created using Unity 3D and Unreal game engines. C# and C++ are my main coding languages but I can code with Python aswell.

My enthusiasm for RPGs and their immersion mechanics has grown into a passion for devloping and improving these systems in games. Been playing games since the Playstation 1, but over time I experienced Xbox, Nintendo and PC gaming. At 16, I created my first game which was a scrollable tank destroyer in c++ aided by SFML.

I am in search of a stimulating and engaging work environment that allows me to foster personal growth and enhance my skills. My aim is to contribute effectively to the company's success, evolving into a more valuable asset for the organization.


Education

University

Between 2020 and 2023 I studied Games Technology at University of The West of England. I became educated in games development, studying games design theory, games programming and the use of developer programs like Autodesk Maya, Unreal, Unity, Visual Studio, Adobe Suite. As well as C#, C++ and Python.

College

In college I studied computer science, maths, physics, and engineering. I implemented my knowledge in these fields to recreate realistic physics for my games development.

Experiences

At uni I worked within groups by being a part of multiple group orientated game jam based tasks, taking a leadership role (for the games design module), and received 6 months of exposure to a development studio environment when making a full PC and PS5 game as a cohort.

Covid deeply affected 2 years of my study at university. However, it provided an experience of working remotely to strict deadlines. I can now communicate efficiently and use multiple types of communication programs, such as, Trello, Slack, Teams and Discord.

Being Polish myself and having experienced multiple cultures, such as spending 9 months in Thailand. I'm excited to cooperate with diverse people and adapt my experiences into interesting and unique game design ideas.
